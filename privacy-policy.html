Privacy Policy — Neon Slice

Last updated: 21 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Neon Slice ("the App", "we") handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not require an account, does not collect your name, email, or any personal contact information, and does not access your contacts, camera, or location.

Your level progress and star ratings are stored only on your own device and are never sent to us.

2. Advertising

The App displays ads served by Google AdMob. To show relevant ads, Google AdMob and its partners may collect and use device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID), approximate location, and usage data, in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization").

3. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

4. Data Storage

Game progress (unlocked levels, star ratings, high score) is stored locally on your device using standard on-device storage. Uninstalling the App removes this data.

5. Third-Party Services

The App loads typography from Google Fonts (a content delivery network) when an internet connection is available. Google Fonts may log standard server request data (such as IP address) as described in Google Fonts' privacy FAQ.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
